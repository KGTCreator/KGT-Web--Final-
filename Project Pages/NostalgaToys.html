Description: Did you ever want to buy a classic, wooden toy that you can share with you kids? Well, Little Bird Toys is for you! This site was made to promote a fictional toy company that specializes in making timeless wooden toys. It was the last project Kalen worked on for the 2022-2023 school year.
Fun Fact: The story behind the company was mostly filled in by Kalen himself, who filled in the blanks from the resources he received. Along with that, this was the first, and for a while only, website Kalen made with a functioning carousel!
