Description: Born from one of Kalen's favorite foods, Cookies Galore covers the history of multiple of the most well known and obscure cookies across the world. Nine cookies are listed here, ranging from the legendary Chocolate Chip, all the way to the lower rated Triggel. Even Kalen learned a thing or two while creating this website, mostly seen in the section dedicated to the Fortune Cookie.
Fun Fact: The obscure section was the hardest to fill out. This is because Kalen had to go through multiple websites about cookies to find the right choices, yet he could normally find ones that he at least heard about. So he had to dig deeper. Thus, all the cookies listed in the obscure section were from the lowest rated cookies in the world.
