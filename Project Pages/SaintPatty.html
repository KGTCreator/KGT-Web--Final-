Visit The Website
Created: March 17, 2023
Type: Extra-Type
Description: In the spirit of St Patrick's Day, this website was made to promote a fictional lottery system based in Ireland. Kalen went through the extra effort of giving the titular lottery company a backstory, with a lucky Leprechaun named Patrick Leppy, and his want to give good luck to as many people as possible. It was a fun little project that included small refrences to Ireland and a few sponsoring companies as well.
Fun Fact: The name, Patrick Leppy, was derived from two separate sources. "Patrick" came from St Patrick himself. While "Leppy" was based on the name of a character made by Fusion Animations, for his small St Patty's Day video, The Little Leprechaun of Seattle.
